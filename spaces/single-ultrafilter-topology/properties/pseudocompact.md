Since $F$ is a free ultrafilter, there is some infinite $A \in F$ with infinite complement in $\omega$. Let $f: X \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ by $f(x)=0$ if $x \in A$ and $f(x)=x$ otherwise. Then $f$ is continuous and unbounded.
