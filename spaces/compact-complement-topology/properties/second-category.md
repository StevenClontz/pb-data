For any $n \in \omega$, let $I_n$ denote the interval $(n, n+2)$. $\overline {I_n} = [n, n+2]$ and $int (\overline{I_n}) = \emptyset$ since any open set in $X$ is unbounded. Clearly $X = \bigcup_{n \in \omega} I_n$, so $X$ is first category.
