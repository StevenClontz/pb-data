Let $\{U_\alpha\}$ be an open cover of $Y$. For each $x_r \in M$, select $U_r$ with $x_r \in U_r \in \{U_\alpha\}$. Let $V_r = \{x \in U_r\ |\ \{r\} \in x\} = U_r \cap \pi^{-1}(1)\}$. Then $\{V_r\} \cup \{\{f\}\ |\ f \in F\}$ is a point-finite open refinement of $\{U_\alpha\}$.
