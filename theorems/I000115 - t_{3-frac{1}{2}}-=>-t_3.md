If $f:X \rightarrow [0,1]$ with $f(a)=0$ and $f(B)=\{1\}$ for some point $a$ and closed set $B$, then $O_a = f^{-1}([0,\frac{1}{2}))$ and $O_B = f^{-1}((\frac{1}{2},1])$ are disjoint open sets containing $a$ and $B$ respectively.
