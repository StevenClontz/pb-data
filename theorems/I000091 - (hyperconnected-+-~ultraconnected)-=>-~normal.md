---
uid: I000091
antecedent:
  and:
  - hyperconnected: true
  - ultraconnected: false
consequent:
  normal: false
---
If $X$ is not ultraconnected, there are two disjoint closed subsets $C$ and $D$. If $X$ is hyperconnected, there cannot be two disjoint open sets containing $C$ and $D$, so $X$ is not normal.
