If $X = \cup_{n \in \omega} C_n$ with each $C_n$ compact and $\mathcal{U}$ is any open cover, then some finite subcollection $\mathcal{U}_n$ covers each $C_n$ and $\cup_n \mathcal{U}_n$ is a countable subcover of $\mathcal{U}$.
