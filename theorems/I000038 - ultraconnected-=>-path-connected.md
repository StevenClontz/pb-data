If $X$ is ultraconnected and $a,b \in X$ then $cl(\{a\}) \cap cl(\{b\}) \neq \emptyset$ so let $p \in cl(\{a\}) \cap cl(\{b\})$. The map $f:[0,1] \rightarrow X$ by $f([0,\frac{1}{2})) = \{a\}$, $f(\frac{1}{2})=p$ and $f((\frac{1}{2},1])=\{b\}$ is continuous.
